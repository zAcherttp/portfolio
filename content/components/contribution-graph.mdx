## Usage

Compose the calendar, blocks, footer, total, and legend to control the graph without replacing its data and interaction model.

```tsx title="contribution-graph.tsx" {3}
<ContributionGraph data={activity} weekStart={1}>
  <ContributionGraphCalendar>
    {(cell) => <ContributionGraphBlock {...cell} />}
  </ContributionGraphCalendar>
</ContributionGraph>
```
